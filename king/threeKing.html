The Three Kingdoms Warlords

Welcome to the real world of the Three Kingdoms, where you can command to your heart's content, fight the deer and conquer the world. After logging in, players will enter their own city, where they will have three types of soldiers: archers, cavalry and infantry, with a total of seven heroes. The player will initially have two heroes by default, and they can be upgraded in the home page of the Government House and Training Grounds. Your own experience and ability value, accumulating coins during the battle, will allow players to buy the items they need in the mall, over the After level 10, you can recruit your own heroes and generals. When you enter the battle, the main city will display the avatar of your hero according to the hero you have, click on it and select the corresponding soldier type, then the hero can lead the soldier to battle. You can only play twice, and the heroes will encounter the corresponding enemies, and you have to eliminate all of them to win.
